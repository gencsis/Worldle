Welcome to Worldle!

Here is how to play :

  • Guess the country based of the image shown on the screen.
  • If you can't guess the country with the first image you can request another.
  • But beware! You will lose points for every new image requested.
  • If you think you know the answer, pinpoint on the map your guess.
  • Complete all three rounds, you win!
